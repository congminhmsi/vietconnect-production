💡 Tips & Best Practices

🚀 Maximizing Earnings

  • Target Quality Referrals: Focus on users likely to be active and engage with the platform
  • Use Multiple Channels: Share your referral links across social media, email, and other platforms
  • Monitor Performance: Regularly check your dashboard to identify top-performing strategies
  • Understand Conditions: Familiarize yourself with all reward conditions to maximize earnings
  • Build Networks: Encourage your referrals to become affiliates themselves

⚠️ Important Guidelines

  • Comply with Terms: Always follow platform terms of service and affiliate guidelines
  • Be Transparent: Disclose affiliate relationships when sharing referral links
  • Avoid Spam: Don't send unsolicited referral links or engage in spammy practices
  • Keep KYC Updated: Ensure your account verification is current for payouts
  • Monitor Regulations: Stay compliant with local laws regarding affiliate marketing
